The Challenge

JACO Arabia required a massive streaming tournament where ten \"kingdoms\" would battle for a single throne. The primary challenge was creating a distinct, high-fidelity visual universe that seamlessly fused modern tactical gear with traditional Middle Eastern attire across 10 unique team identities, all while operating on a highly compressed production timeline.

The Solution

I pioneered a \"Desert Cyberpunk\" / \"Neo-Arabia\" aesthetic using a highly technical AI-augmented workflow. By combining meticulous AI master prompt engineering with advanced digital compositing and painting, I achieved photorealistic, atmospheric depth. This workflow reduced concept turnaround time by 40% and delivered a highly engaging, narrative-driven IP for the platform's top streamers.
