Challenge

JACO Arabia needed to drive growth across a live-streaming platform in a highly competitive MENA market. The challenge: design three sequential, performance-optimized campaigns that would increase daily active users, drive community engagement, and demonstrate measurable ROI while maintaining creative excellence and brand consistency.

The Problem
  • Competitive live-streaming market (high user acquisition costs)
  • Need for rapid iteration based on performance data
  • Multiple campaign sequences with dependencies
  • Maintaining creative quality at high velocity
  • Measuring and optimizing for real ROI (not vanity metrics)
The Solution
  • Data-driven creative testing framework
  • Modular campaign design for rapid iteration
  • Integrated analytics across all channels
  • Real-time optimization cycles (weekly/daily)
  • Performance-first creative direction without sacrificing craft

Campaign Strategy

The Growth Trilogy consisted of three integrated campaigns, each building on previous performance learnings and designed to achieve specific growth milestones while maintaining creative cohesion.

Campaign One: Awareness

💬
Campaign Two: Engagement

🚀
Campaign Three: Conversion
Performance-optimized push converting engaged audience into active users and premium subscribers with clear value proposition

Integrated Analytics
Unified metrics dashboard tracking all KPIs across campaigns, enabling real-time optimization and data-driven creative decisions

Strategic Insights

  • Creative + Data = Growth: Combining strategic art direction with real-time performance optimization outperformed either approach alone
  • Sequential messaging works: Three-phase campaign architecture (Awareness → Engagement → Conversion) aligned with natural user journey and improved conversion efficiency
  • Community as multiplier: Leveraging creators and organic sharing amplified paid campaign reach and reduced per-acquisition costs
  • Premium + Performance: Maintained brand aesthetic and creative excellence while aggressively optimizing for performance metrics—no false choice required
  • Velocity matters: Weekly optimization cycles allowed rapid iteration and enabled quick pivots to capitalize on emerging trends
  • 91x ROI is sustainable: Exceptional ROAS achieved through structural improvements, not unsustainable volume plays—established foundation for ongoing growth
