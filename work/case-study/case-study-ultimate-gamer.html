The Opportunity

Following the Esports World Cup, JACO faced a critical moment: millions of passive viewers from the broadcast represented untapped creator and revenue potential. The challenge was to convert that transient audience into a sustained, engaged community IP with recurring participation, monetization, and streamer loyalty.

Core Challenge
  • 3.8M EWC live viewers—mostly passive spectators
  • No mechanism to convert broadcast engagement into platform stickiness
  • Gaming creators needed incentive structure to participate & stream regularly
  • No competitive "event IP" to drive recurring seasonal engagement
  • Revenue opportunity untapped; sponsors seeking gaming audience activation
Strategic Solution
  • Launched #UltimateGamer as recurring seasonal "Broadcast-to-Engage-to-Empower" IP
  • Designed multi-track, gamified competition structure with genre diversity (FPS, MOBA, BR, GTA, Sports)
  • Built escalating prize pools ($22K S1 → $430K+ total S2 coins)
  • Integrated sponsor partnerships (PUBG MOBILE evolution: bonus pilot → core track)
  • Implemented data-driven governance & retention strategies for S3

Three-Season Evolution

#UltimateGamer evolved through three distinct seasons, each informed by performance data and strategic learnings. The progression shows how creative leadership scales a gaming IP from pilot to powerhouse.

Season 1: Proof of Concept
May – June 2025 • Month-long Festival
Participants: 5,000 creators
Prize Pool: $22,000
Tracks: 10 genre-specific competitions (FPS, MOBA, GTA, Sports, etc.)
Duration: Main event 4 weeks + PUBG Bonus (+1 week)

Key Insight: Established proof that passive viewers convert to active creators. PUBG MOBILE "Bonus" pilot performed well, proving sponsor integration viability.
Season 2: Scaling & Integration
Oct – Nov 2025 • Growth Phase
Performance: $58.5K revenue peak (single week), 838K launch views
Prize Pool: 408,000 JACO coins (~$4K per track)
Tracks: 8 core tracks + PUBG Mobile fully integrated

Critical Finding: Engagement dropped Weeks 45–46 as leaderboards solidified. Users disengaged when they felt mathematically eliminated.
Season 3: Retention Reimagined
Feb 2026 (Ramadan Launch) • Sustainability
Strategy: Point System replacing leaderboard elimination
Mechanic: 1,000 points per 60 minutes of streaming (regardless of rank)
Governance: Strict anti-fraud rules (idle streaming penalties, multi-account bans)

Design Philosophy: Remove "dead weight" feeling. Keep engagement constant through consistent reward accumulation.

Season 2: Scaling & Revenue Peak (Oct–Nov 2025)

S2 saw 3x growth in prize pools, PUBG MOBILE upgrade from "bonus" to core track, and peak revenue of $58.5K in a single week. However, data revealed critical engagement drop-off mid-season.

S2 Key Learning: "Leaderboard fatigue" drove late-season engagement drop. Once rankings felt locked, creators stopped streaming. This insight directly led to S3's point-system redesign, which rewards consistency rather than cumulative ranking position.

PUBG MOBILE Partnership Arc

The PUBG MOBILE sponsorship evolved from a limited experiment to a core partnership, demonstrating how strategic brand integration scales across campaign seasons.

🎮
Phase 1: Bonus Pilot (S1)
7-day limited "Bonus Category" promoting Battle Royale & Metro Royale modes. Viewership-based scoring to maximize marketing reach. $2K modest prize pool. Result: Strong performance validated sponsor integration model.
📈
Phase 2: Full Integration (S2)
Graduated to core track: "Most Active PUBG Mobile Streamer." Scoring shifted to activity/duration (top performer logged 180K+ duration units). Prize pool increased to 408K coins (~$4K), matching FPS/MOBA. Result: Legitimized as headline partnership.
🏆
Phase 3: Ongoing (S3+)
Continued as core track with new game focus (FC26 soccer game added). Demonstrates sponsor confidence in IP sustainability. Sets model for future gaming partners to onboard into #UltimateGamer ecosystem.
💡
Strategic Value
Sponsor partnerships validate IP credibility, justify prize pools, and create revenue streams. PUBG MOBILE's three-phase evolution from test to core track shows art direction's role in building sponsor confidence through execution quality.

Creative Execution & Design System

Across all three seasons, consistent creative direction anchored the brand while adapting to evolving mechanics and sponsor needs.

🎨
Visual Identity
Neon-cyan glow aesthetic (purple + cyan accents) on dark backgrounds. Bold geometric logo with controller icon. "LET THE STREAM BEGIN!" / "LEVEL UP THE STREAM" taglines. Consistent across broadcast graphics, in-app H5s, social banners, and sponsor assets.
📱
Multi-Platform Delivery
In-app leaderboard UI with ranked tier visuals (gold/silver/bronze crown badges). Mobile H5 tutorials ("How to: Join & Stream"). Home & discovery banners optimized for platform placement. Updated branded assets for each season launch.
🌍
Localization
Bilingual AR/EN throughout. Genre-specific track naming (FPS, MOBA, GTA, Sports Game, BR). PUBG Mobile asset integration preserving partner brand hierarchy. Rules & reward messaging clear across all regional users.
⚡
Adaptive Design
S2 expanded track count (8 tracks) without breaking UI hierarchy. S3 pivot to point system required leaderboard redesign. Creative system flexible enough to accommodate sponsor evolution and mechanical changes mid-season.

Season 3: Data-Driven Retention Strategy (Feb 2026 Launch)

Armed with S2 engagement data, S3 pivots to a point-accumulation system designed to eliminate leaderboard fatigue and maintain consistent creator participation throughout the season.

The Problem Solved: S2 leaderboards solidified by mid-season, causing creators to disengage once they felt mathematically out of contention. S3 removes this barrier by rewarding streaming consistency (1,000 points per 60 minutes) regardless of rank position.

Governance & Integrity: New anti-fraud rules impose penalties for "idle streaming" (broadcasting without active gameplay) and multi-account farming. Strict enforcement protects leaderboard credibility and prevents point manipulation.
Launch Timing: S3 launches February 17–18 (1st day of Ramadan), strategically aligning with peak streaming activity during fasting month when creators seek evening engagement.

What This Demonstrates

  • Broadcast-to-Engage Model: Converted 3.8M passive viewers into 5K+ active creators; proved sustainable seasonal IP viability
  • Data-Driven Creative Leadership: Used S2 engagement drop (Weeks 45–46) to redesign S3 mechanics. Art direction informed by performance analytics
  • Sponsor Integration Strategy: Evolved PUBG MOBILE from pilot bonus to core track with 3x prize increase. Shows ability to build partner confidence through execution
  • Multi-Season Creative Consistency: Maintained visual identity & brand clarity across 3 seasons while adapting UI/UX to evolving mechanics
  • Retention-Focused Design: Identified leaderboard fatigue; redesigned S3 with point system to eliminate engagement cliff. Proactive, not reactive
  • Cross-Functional Leadership: Coordinated with Gaming, Content Ops, and Marketing teams. Managed creative briefs, asset delivery, and sponsor communications
  • Gaming IP Architecture: Built genre-diverse track structure (FPS, MOBA, BR, Sports, GTA) enabling multiple creator communities to participate in single IP
  • Revenue Leadership: $58.5K/week peak revenue represents direct monetization impact of creative strategy execution

Key Learnings & Future Roadmap

📊
S2→S3 Learning
Leaderboard psychology matters. Once rankings solidify, creators disengage. Point accumulation systems keep participation consistent throughout season length.
🎮
Genre Diversification Works
10+ distinct tracks across FPS, MOBA, BR, GTA, Sports attracted different creator communities. No single-genre fatigue; healthy ecosystem diversity.
💎
Sponsor Partnerships Scale
PUBG MOBILE's 3-phase evolution (bonus → core → headline) proves partners invest in proven IPs. Sets template for future brand integrations.
🔐
Governance Matters
S3's anti-fraud rules (idle streaming penalties, multi-account bans) protect credibility. Necessary to maintain creator trust and leaderboard integrity at scale.
S3+ Roadmap: Continue point-system testing through Ramadan. Measure retention vs. S2 baseline. If successful, establish #UltimateGamer as permanent quarterly IP. Expand sponsor ecosystem. Introduce creator tier system (bronze/silver/gold levels) for aspirational progression. Explore cross-game integration (linking scores across multiple titles).
