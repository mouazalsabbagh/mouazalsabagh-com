Campaign Challenge

The Ministry of Energy needed to establish Saudi Arabia as a credible, forward-thinking leader in global energy transition conversations during critical international summits. The challenge required translating complex energy policy into compelling visual narratives while managing tight production timelines across multiple concurrent global events.

Strategic Challenges
  • Position Kingdom as active participant in global climate discourse
  • Communicate energy policy to international audiences in real-time
  • Manage multiple simultaneous events (COP 27, COP 28, Petroleum Congress)
  • Translate complex technical content into accessible visual narratives
  • Establish thought leadership amid competing national agendas
  • Scale production to meet high-volume, rapid-turnaround demands
Creative Solution
  • Developed integrated content strategy across visual, video, and infographic formats
  • Built rapid-response production system enabling same-day asset delivery
  • Created reusable design templates for consistent brand identity
  • Designed modular content architecture for multi-platform distribution
  • Implemented data visualization system for policy translation
  • Produced 100+ high-quality assets across three concurrent summits

Summit Coverage Strategy

The Ministry's presence across three major global energy forums—COP 27 (2022), COP 28 (2023), and the 24th World Petroleum Congress (2023)—required distinct content strategies while maintaining unified brand positioning.

Summit Dates Focus Area Key Content Themes
COP 27 Nov–Dec 2022 Climate action leadership Saudi contributions to climate mitigation, renewable energy initiatives, national policy positioning
COP 28 Nov–Dec 2023 Energy transition narratives Emissions reduction pathways, renewable energy deployment, diversification strategy

Key Strategic Learnings

  • → Video-first strategy maximizes reach: 52M video views vs. traditional static content demonstrates audience preference for visual storytelling. Complex policy communicated more effectively through visual narratives than text alone.
  • → Bilingual execution is essential: Arabic/English dual-language design enabled simultaneous regional and international audience engagement. Single-language approach would have limited reach to half target audience.
  • → Data visualization simplifies complex messaging: Infographics translating policy complexity into visual format increased comprehension and shareability. Technical content became accessible to diverse audiences.
  • → Modular design enables scale: Reusable templates and component libraries enabled 100+ asset production without quality degradation. Systematic approach to creative production maintained consistency at volume.
  • → Real-time responsiveness drives engagement: Same-day asset delivery for summit announcements enabled rapid message amplification. Agility in production schedule significantly enhanced campaign effectiveness.
