The Challenge

JACO was growing sponsor partnerships aggressively (5–8 active sponsors per quarter) but faced a critical design bottleneck that threatened to limit revenue acceleration. Every new sponsor required 6–8 weeks of custom asset creation, causing sales team delays, sponsor frustration, and missed revenue opportunities.

Core Business Problem
  • 6–8 week sponsor onboarding cycle delayed deal closure
  • Inconsistent asset treatment across platforms (social, email, in-app, broadcast)
  • Each sponsor required custom design work—no reusable components
  • Sales team blocked waiting for assets before sponsor activation
  • Sponsors had no visibility into deliverables or timelines
  • Design team capacity maxed at 5–8 concurrent sponsors
Systematic Solution
  • Architected JACO Business Kit—comprehensive brand system with templated workflow
  • Designed 50+ editable templates across 6 categories (social, email, in-app, broadcast, events, print)
  • Standardized 3-tier sponsor packaging (Tier 1/2/3) with defined deliverables
  • Built modular component library for consistent, native-feeling sponsor assets
  • Created 60-page Business Kit documentation + 8-video training series
  • Established QA workflow enabling junior designer autonomy
