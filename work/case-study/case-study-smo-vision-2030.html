Challenge

The Strategic Management Office (SMO) needed to amplify Vision 2030 initiatives across Saudi Arabia with maximum impact and consistency. The challenge: coordinate 16+ simultaneous campaigns across digital, print, broadcast, and environmental channels in 10+ major cities—all while maintaining unified brand messaging and visual identity for a complex, multi-stakeholder governmental initiative.

The Problem
  • Fragmented campaign execution across regions
  • Inconsistent visual messaging
  • Need for rapid asset production at scale
  • Complex stakeholder alignment requirements
  • High-stakes public communication context
The Approach
  • Centralized creative direction framework
  • Modular campaign architecture
  • Standardized asset production pipeline
  • Real-time brand compliance system
  • Integrated performance tracking

Key Learnings

This project reinforced critical insights about large-scale governmental communications:

  • Centralization drives consistency: A single creative authority prevented message fragmentation across regions and channels
  • Modular design scales: Component-based asset creation allowed rapid production without sacrificing quality
  • Governance matters: Formal approval workflows with stakeholder input accelerated decision-making
  • Data informs iteration: Real-time performance metrics allowed continuous optimization of creative approaches
  • Vision 2030 resonates: Aligned messaging with national strategic priorities amplified campaign effectiveness
