Campaign Challenge

The Ehsan Platform faced a critical challenge: digitizing charitable giving during key religious seasons (Ramadan, Dhul-Hijjah) while establishing absolute trust in a newly unified national platform. The core difficulty was shifting deeply ingrained traditional, offline philanthropic behaviors to a secure, modern digital ecosystem without sacrificing the emotional resonance that drives charitable action.

Strategic Challenges
  • Shift offline charitable behaviors to digital platform
  • Establish absolute trust in newly unified national system
  • Maintain emotional resonance throughout digitization
  • Overcome skepticism around digital payment security
  • Maximize giving during concentrated seasonal windows
  • Create daily lifestyle habit beyond seasonal campaigns
Creative Solution
  • Developed "Transparent Compassion" visual identity
  • Launched high-profile national campaigns with Royal Family endorsements
  • Executed emotionally resonant top-tier video production
  • Designed frictionless UI/UX enabling seconds-to-donation
  • Integrated trusted payment methods (Apple Pay, Mada)
  • Created comprehensive data visualization for transparency

Visual Identity: "Transparent Compassion"

The visual strategy balanced solemnity and dignity of charitable work with modern, premium digital aesthetics. The identity needed to communicate security and trust while remaining emotionally resonant and accessible to all demographics.

🎨
Color System
Primary: Deep, trustworthy blues (security, stability) | Secondary: Vibrant teals (accessibility, warmth) | Emotional: Warm earth tones connecting to human stories
📸
Authentic Storytelling
Photography and cinematography focused strictly on authentic, human-centric narratives. Avoided overly staged imagery. Featured real beneficiaries and donors creating genuine emotional connections.
✨
Premium Minimalism
Extreme clarity, warmth, and minimalism across all touchpoints. Design language emphasized that giving felt both profound and completely effortless. Premium execution without complexity.
🤝
Trust Building
Every design element reinforced platform security and transparency. Visual hierarchy emphasized beneficiary stories over transaction mechanics. Holistic experience building emotional confidence.

Campaign Architecture: Seasonal Maximization

Ehsan's strategy leveraged key religious seasons with tailored campaigns while building year-round engagement. The "National Campaign for Charitable Work" and "Dhul-Hijjah Campaign" demonstrated how strategic timing multiplies impact.

Campaign Timing Strategic Focus Impact Approach
Ramadan Campaign Holy Month (High Giving) Spiritual motivation at peak engagement Emotional video narratives, Royal endorsements, real beneficiary stories
Dhul-Hijjah Campaign Hajj Season (Religious Peak) Pilgrimage-connected giving impulse Premium performance reporting, transparency communication, gratitude narratives
Year-Round Engagement Daily Lifestyle Integration Habit formation beyond seasonal spikes Regular beneficiary stories, impact updates, community celebrating giving
