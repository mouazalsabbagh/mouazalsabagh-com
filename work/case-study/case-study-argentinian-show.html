Client

Ministry of Culture

Scope

Show Promotion

Year

01

Overview

The Argentinian Show positioned an international theatre production as a cultural moment that bridged Argentina and Saudi Arabia. Working with TPAC and Dozan, we created comprehensive promotional and brand materials that drove audience engagement for this significant cultural exchange event.

The campaign encompassed show branding, performer promotion, audience development, and digital marketing.

02

Deliverables

Show visual identity and promotional materials, performer highlight assets, audience engagement campaigns, ticketing promotion, and social media strategy that elevated the international production into a cultural event.
