Campaign Challenge

Saudi Tourism Authority faced critical perception challenge: shifting global and domestic understanding of Saudi Arabia from traditionally religious/business travel destination to premier, year-round tourist attraction with diverse experiences.

Perception Barriers
  • Global perception limited to religious pilgrimage
  • Limited awareness of natural/cultural diversity
  • Business travel vs. leisure gap
  • International misconceptions about accessibility
  • Competing destination awareness
  • Overcoming decades of limited tourism marketing
Strategic Positioning
  • "Breathtaking Diversity" positioning highlighting varied geography
  • Global ambassadors (Lionel Messi) building international credibility
  • Hyper-premium cinematic storytelling showcasing heritage sites
  • Multi-channel campaigns (TV, OOH, digital, social) integrated globally
  • Localized messaging ("Rethink Summer") for seasonal activation
  • Visual contrast: earthy heritage + vibrant modern lifestyle

Visual Identity: "Breathtaking Diversity"

The visual strategy emphasized hyper-premium cinematography contrasting majestic, earthy tones of Saudi heritage with vibrant, modern lifestyle coloring. Identity needed to communicate world-class tourism while maintaining authentic cultural reverence.

🏜️
Heritage Cinematography
AlUla's historic streets, Asir's cool mountain landscapes, Red Sea resorts. Macro-scale landscape shots emphasizing geological grandeur and cultural authenticity.
🌴
Lifestyle Integration
Modern hospitality, adventure activities, cultural experiences. Vibrant coloring contrasting with earthy heritage tones creating inviting tourism aesthetic.
🌍
Global Appeal
Bilingual typography ensuring international accessibility while maintaining local authenticity. Premium production values positioning Saudi Arabia as world-class destination.
👥
Ambassador Strategy
Global figures (Lionel Messi) paired with Saudi influencers. Personal authenticity and cultural relevance driving both international and domestic engagement.

Campaign Execution

Multi-channel strategy leveraging international broadcast, premium OOH placement, immersive digital experiences, and localized social campaigns to drive visa applications and tourism spending.

📺
Global TVC
Television commercials with professional storyboards, cinematic visual treatments showcasing Saudi geography and modern tourism infrastructure at premium production levels.
🛣️
OOH Strategy
High-resolution billboard layouts in international airports, major global cities. Premium placement reaching business travelers and international audiences at scale.
💻
Digital Experience
Immersive digital display ads, interactive web banners, virtual destination experiences. User engagement metrics tracking tourism inquiry conversions.
📱
Social Localization
Motion graphics, video cutdowns, "Rethink Summer" seasonal campaigns. Localized messaging for domestic markets, global amplification for international reach.
Impact: Massive spike in tourist visa applications, significantly boosted domestic travel spending, successfully reshaped global narrative positioning Saudi Arabia as premier tourist destination.

Strategic Outcomes

The Saudi Tourism Authority campaign demonstrated how premium creative positioning can fundamentally reshape national perception and drive measurable tourism outcomes.

  • → Global Narrative Shift: Successfully repositioned Saudi Arabia from religious/business destination to world-class tourist hub with diverse experiences and modern hospitality.
  • → International Credibility: Global ambassador partnerships (Lionel Messi) communicated world-class status and international appeal to skeptical markets.
  • → Visa Application Impact: Measurable spike in tourist visa applications demonstrating campaign effectiveness and genuine market interest conversion.
  • → Vision 2030 Alignment: Direct support for economic diversification away from oil, enabling tourism sector growth as major revenue and employment driver.
