Heritage & Banan Exhibition

Cultural Heritage Positioning and Exhibition Design for Saudi Arabia's Artistic Legacy

Client

Ministry of Culture

Partner

Biio

Scope

Exhibition Design & Branding

Year

2023–2024

01

Overview

The Heritage & Banan Exhibition represented a critical cultural initiative to celebrate Saudi Arabia's artistic and historical legacy. Working alongside Biio and the Ministry of Culture, we developed comprehensive visual identity and exhibition design that positioned cultural heritage as a contemporary, engaging experience rather than a static archive.

The project encompassed exhibition layout, wayfinding systems, promotional materials, and digital assets that unified the cultural narrative across multiple touchpoints.

02

Deliverables

Exhibition design and visual systems, promotional campaign assets, wayfinding and environmental graphics, digital marketing content, and brand positioning materials that elevated cultural heritage into the contemporary cultural conversation.
